What Is It?

A list-making tool specifically designed around the daily tasks you find in everyday games. We know that people get busy throughout the day and sometimes forget to have fun when the real-life tasks are over. Gamer Dailies will help keep you on track so you don't miss out. Need a space to list everything you need to do in a single day of Stardew Valley? Need a daily reminder so you don't loose your login streak? Or maybe you just need a dedicated space to plan out your next Minecraft project. Whatever you need, Gamer Dailies will be there to help.
